Congratulations, Class of 2020

Welcome to the 2020 EVMS Virtual Commencement — home for this year's online ceremony and the go-to-place for celebrating the graduating class.

The COVID-19 pandemic has impacted us all, but it certainly does not diminish the achievements of our graduates. We appreciate the EVMS community coming together to celebrate the Class of 2020 and encourage you to join us in recognizing their accomplishments.
